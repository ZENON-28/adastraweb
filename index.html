// 01. WHO WE ARE

Forging the Future,
One Build at a Time

Ad Astra is more than a student club — it is a launchpad for the next generation of engineers, designers, and innovators who dare to push the boundaries of what's possible.

Our community of students works at the intersection of robotics, aerospace, artificial intelligence, and embedded systems — building real systems, winning competitions, and shipping projects that matter.

We believe the best learning happens when you're in the lab, hands on hardware, testing under pressure. From autonomous rovers to drone swarms, we build what others only dream of.
